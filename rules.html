Gameplay Structure

  • 9 balls per frame
  • 10 frames per match
  • Lowest scoring frame is dropped from total match score
  • Elite Series matches: 10 frames head-to-head
  • Open Division matches: 5 frames head-to-head

Lane Specifications

FILC competition lanes must meet the following requirements:

  • Length: Between 13 and 15 feet
  • Seven scoring rings:
    • 2x 100-point cups (upper corners)
    • 1x 50-point cup (center)
    • 1x 40, 1x 30, 1x 20, 1x 10 (lower arc)
  • 10-foot lanes are permitted for recreational play only and not valid for official rankings or competition

Match Formats

All matches are head-to-head. Players alternate frames unless otherwise specified in tournament rules.

  • Elite Series: 10-frame matches
  • All other FILC tournaments: 5-frame matches
  • Tiebreakers: One-ball sudden death (highest value wins; tie = repeat)

Rule Variants

  • Shot Clock Mode: Players have 30 seconds per ball; missed shots are scored as zero
  • Target Lock: Players must declare a target ring (e.g. 100 or 50) and hit it at least once per frame or face a 10-point deduction
  • Reverse Frame: Points scored are inverted (e.g. 100 = 10); used in select exhibitions
  • Sudden Roll Format: First to break a cumulative score barrier (e.g. 500) wins
